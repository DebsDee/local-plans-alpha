The Digital Land team and policymakers at the Ministry of Housing, Communities and Local Government (MHCLG) have built a national dataset of local plans. We collect local plan data from local planning authorities and run it through our pipeline process, which updates the data to meet the standard and fixes common errors in the data.

Whilst our process has significantly improved the data, we cannot guarantee its accuracy, since it relies on the data published by the local planning authorities.
